Source title: Ohio State What To Bring
Source type: official OSU housing page
URL: https://housing.osu.edu/resources/what-to-bring/

Ohio State says all regular residence halls are outfitted with beds, a desk, a desk chair, window covering, a trash can or recycle bin, a closet or wardrobe unit, and a refrigerator/microwave unit. This means students usually do not need to bring those basic furniture or appliance items separately for regular residence halls.

The page also says Ohio State provides a general what-to-bring list and recommends that students consult with their roommate or roommates to avoid duplicates. This is important practical advice because dorm rooms have limited space and duplicate items can make a room crowded.
